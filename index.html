Have these ready before you start — the form works best when completed in one session.

  1. Take a photo of your Medical License on this device and save it to your Photos or Files.
  2. Take a photo of your Malpractice Certificate of Insurance on this device and save it to your Photos or Files.
  3. Upload each page of your CV as a photo or scan. All pages will be included in your PDF receipt — no separate file needed.
  4. Complete all sections of the form, then press "Submit Form."
  5. Press "Save Receipt as PDF." ⚠️ iOS: tap the Share ↗ symbol (circle with arrow) — it appears at the top right of the print preview screen. Do not tap the blue printer icon.
  6. Email the saved PDF to UCLA: MVela@mednet.ucla.edu — Your CV pages are already included in the PDF.
  7. To save a copy for your own records: press "Save Receipt as PDF" again, then at the top of the print preview choose either the Share ↗ button on the left to save to Files, or the blue Print button on the right to print a hard copy.
